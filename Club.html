Meeting Agenda for the Club

CS Programming club bring together the computer science community at Salem State. Many students develop a strong sense of support, belonging, and camaraderie by joining and leading these clubs. We encourage you to get involved.

Meeting Agenda for the Club

The agenda items discussed in each meeting may look a little different based on the attendance and members. The meeting Agenda included:

  • 1.Introduction
    • • Introduce yourselves: the Presidents, the sponsor, etc.
    • • Do an ice-breaker activity or short conversion
  • 2. Introduce the Movement and purpose of the club
    • • Explain what the Movement is (show a video or use website to assist).
    • • Give an overview of what the rest of the meetings may look like. This is your opportunity to cast vision for the future of the club!
  • 3. Introduce the focus of this meeting and dive right into discussing the topic(Spring 2021: Python Programming and Fall2021 web programming)
  • 4. Make Announcements:
    • • Remind group members to check Facebook, Instagram, emails, etc. for future announcements • Remind them of future meetings (provide a calendar or dates for the next meeting)
    • • Encourage members to invite their friends
    • • Suggest service opportunities in the community for the week
    • • Brainstorm ideas for service projects and fundraising ideas
    • • Provide a handout with the announcements at each meeting
